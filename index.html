About

I started looking at creating SCOM management packs when I found out how expensive some of them could be, and wondered if I could create Management Packs that would give us the information we needed.

I started with a simple F5 Management pack that monitored Certificate Expiration, this rapidly expanded into AP.F5.Base and AP.F5.LTM.
